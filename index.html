Our Story

Dhangadhi's Favorite Cake & Restaurant Spot

Freshness & Quality in Every Single Bite

Located on the bustling Main Road of Dhangadhi, Nepal, Cake & Kitchen has become the town's go-to destination for mouthwatering cakes and wholesome meals. We blend artisan cake crafting and cooking techniques with authentic Nepalese and international flavors to serve our community.

Whether you're looking for an exquisite custom wedding cake, a delightful birthday cake, hot snacks, or a complete restaurant meal, we craft it with love using fresh ingredients. All our items are carefully priced in the Rs 100 – 500 range, making culinary excellence accessible to everyone.
